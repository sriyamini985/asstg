Who We Are

Association of Spine Surgeons of Telangana

A premier state-level professional medical body representing orthopedic spine surgeons in Telangana.

Established as a dedicated society of orthopedic spine surgeons, the Association serves to foster clinical safety, academic excellence, ethical surgical practice, and patient-centric spine care throughout the state of Telangana.

Our Mission

Promote excellence in spine surgery through education, ethical practice, research, collaboration, and professional development.

Our Vision

Build a strong academic and clinical community dedicated to improving spine health and patient outcomes across Telangana.

Memorandum of Association

Aims and Objectives

The foundational directives guiding the growth, ethics, and education of spinal surgery within the state of Telangana.

Promote Spine Education

Ensure consistent updates in training modules for orthopedic spine specialists across regional institutions.

Organize CME Programs

Conduct regular Continuing Medical Education seminars to present clinical findings and discuss advanced spine case studies.

Conduct Workshops

Facilitate hands-on training courses and advanced workshops to enhance the practical skills of spine surgeons.

Support Scientific Research

Channel academic publications and guide members in active clinical research related to spinal implants and techniques.

Encourage Ethical Practice

Uphold ethical guidelines and frame clinical protocols to support patient safety and protect professional surgeon interests.

Strengthen Collaboration

Bridge communication with the government, lawmakers, surgical implant industries, and national forums like ASSI.

Advance Clinical Excellence

Set guidelines for different clinical situations to establish the highest standards of spinal healthcare across the state.

Core Pillars

Our Foundational Values

The Association operates on key professional values designed to support surgical experts, research scholars, and academic leaders.

Education

Providing specialized training courses, hands-on workshops, and certified learning modules to medical graduates and practicing spine specialists.

Research

Supporting high-impact clinical trials, publications, and advancements in bioengineering relevant to spinal implants and surgical navigation.

Collaboration

Fostering academic connections with national forums like IOA, ASSI, and global platforms like AO Spine to exchange clinical knowledge.

Innovation

Integrating robotic surgery protocols, 3D printing modeling, minimally invasive procedures, and artificial intelligence into regional practice.

Why Join the Association?

Membership in the Association of Spine Surgeons of Telangana is a distinction that demonstrates your commitment to quality, academic leadership, and peer alignment in spine surgery.

  • Academic Credentials: Recognition as a certified specialist in regional academic networks.
  • Discounted Registrations: Premium rates for the annual conference and all regional workshops, CMEs.
  • Research Grants: Eligibility to apply for research funding and peer-reviewed journal assistance.
  • Hands-on Training: Priority slots in cadaveric workshops and advanced surgical fellowships.

Who is Eligible?

The Association maintains rigorous qualification standards to preserve the integrity of academic debate and professional healthcare benchmarks.

General Surgeons & Orthopaedic/Neuro Surgeons

Surgeons holding an M.Ch. (Neuro/Spine), D.N.B. (Neuro/Ortho), or M.S. (Orthopaedics) from a recognized university and actively practicing or demonstrating a specialized interest in spinal surgery.

PG Students / Residents

Postgraduate students enrolled in recognized Orthopaedic or Neurosurgical residency programs who wish to access educational symposia.

Membership Structure

Life Member
₹5,000

For qualified MS/DNB/M.Ch spine specialists. Full voting rights, eligible for committees.

Associate Member
₹3,000

For allied professionals, neurosurgeons, research scientists, and other allied specialists (no voting rights, cannot hold office).

Application Process

  1. Fill out the Online Application or PDF Form.
  2. Attach medical council registration, PG certificates, and brief clinical profile.
  3. Provide endorsement signatures from two current Association Life Members.
  4. Pay application fees via bank transfer or online gateway.

Flagship Conference

1st Annual Conference of the Association of Spine Surgeons of Telangana

ASSTCON 2026 is the landmark academic gathering of spinal experts and surgeons in the state, scheduled for September 2026.

Dear Colleagues,

It gives us immense pleasure to welcome you to ASSTCON 2026 – the 1st Annual Conference of the Association of Spine Surgeons of Telangana, scheduled to be held on 27th September 2026 at Hotel Taj Deccan, Hyderabad.

On behalf of the Organizing Committee, we are delighted to invite you to this landmark academic gathering, which marks the beginning of a new journey for our state chapter. The Association has been established with a vision to bring together spine surgeons dedicated to excellence in spine care, education, research, and innovation.

The scientific program of ASSTCON 2026 has been carefully designed to provide an enriching academic experience through expert lectures, video sessions, interactive case discussions, and exchange of clinical knowledge by eminent national and local faculty. This conference aims to encourage learning, inspire young surgeons, promote research, and strengthen collaboration among spine specialists while upholding the highest standards of ethical patient care.
