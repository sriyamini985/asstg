About ASST

United for Spine Health, Driven by Excellence

Established in 2026, the Association of Spine Surgeons of Telangana (ASST) serves as the leading academic and professional society dedicated solely to the advancement of spinal surgery and healthcare standards in Telangana.

Our Mission

To nurture clinical excellence and safety in spine care through structured educational courses, cutting-edge clinical research, and collaborative expert networking.

Our Vision

To establish Telangana as a global hub of innovation, research, and premium ethical standards in spinal healthcare and surgeon education.

Core Pillars

Our Foundational Values

ASST operates on key professional values designed to support surgical experts, research scholars, and academic leaders.

Education

Providing specialized training courses, hands-on workshops, and certified learning modules to medical graduates and practicing spine specialists.

Research

Supporting high-impact clinical trials, publications, and advancements in bioengineering relevant to spinal implants and surgical navigation.

Collaboration

Fostering academic connections with national forums like IOA, ASSI, and global platforms like AO Spine to exchange clinical knowledge.

Innovation

Integrating robotic surgery protocols, 3D printing modeling, minimally invasive procedures, and artificial intelligence into regional practice.

Why Join ASST?

Membership in ASST is a distinction that demonstrates your commitment to quality, academic leadership, and peer alignment in spine surgery.

  • Academic Credentials: Recognition as a certified specialist in regional academic networks.
  • Discounted Registrations: Premium rates for ASSTCON and all regional workshops, CMEs.
  • Research Grants: Eligibility to apply for research funding and peer-reviewed journal assistance.
  • Hands-on Training: Priority slots in cadaveric workshops and advanced surgical fellowships.

Who is Eligible?

ASST maintains rigorous qualification standards to preserve the integrity of academic debate and professional healthcare benchmarks.

General Surgeons & Orthopaedic/Neuro Surgeons

Surgeons holding an M.Ch. (Neuro/Spine), D.N.B. (Neuro/Ortho), or M.S. (Orthopaedics) from a recognized university and actively practicing or demonstrating a specialized interest in spinal surgery.

PG Students / Residents

Postgraduate students enrolled in recognized Orthopaedic or Neurosurgical residency programs who wish to access educational symposia.

About ASST

Association of Spine Surgeons of Telangana

Our History and Purpose

The Association of Spine Surgeons of Telangana (ASST) was formed to address the expanding clinical and academic needs of spine surgery specialists in Telangana. Spine surgery has evolved from a sub-discipline of Orthopaedics and Neurosurgery into a highly specialized field of its own, demanding dedicated platforms for discussion, study, and research.

ASST brings together neurosurgeons and orthopaedic spine surgeons on a single collaborative platform, promoting medical alignment, academic leadership, and patient safety.

Key Objectives

  • Maintain high ethical standards in the delivery of spinal care across the state.
  • Conduct regular clinical meetings, CMEs, symposia, and surgical workshops.
  • Support fellowship training programs for young post-graduates and surgeons.
  • Facilitate academic collaboration with international societies (AO Spine, NASS) and national groups (ASSI).
  • Establish clinical registers to track treatment outcomes and spinal pathology statistics in Telangana.
